4 research findings
  • Users and managers couldn’t see which accessioning resources were available or unavailable.
  • No real-time visibility into accessioning equipment performance, availability, and efficiency.
  • Managers had no real-time view of accessioning backlogs.
  • Supervisors needed a real-time way to dispatch accessioning resources by sample volume.
Shipped
  • A dashboard to monitor accessioning resource availability and performance.
  • TV-mode display of available / unavailable resources.
  • Supervisor dispatch tooling and manager planning tooling.
  • Multi-product flow across ACCN, EXT & HZN, live sample-speed per case, and shift-by-shift milestone gamification.
Process

How it came together

Interviews→Resource UX→Live flow views→Figma · TV mode
  1. User interviews & group discussions to define the problem and empathize with how the lab actually works.
  2. Write PRDs and PSDs capturing user needs and the target workflow.
  3. Review technical feasibility with the POD (LabVantage) against the requirements.
  4. Finalize and prioritize requirements across sprints by team capacity; share knowledge with the Dev team.
  5. Mockups & Figma prototypes → user interviews to capture feedback and finalize the design.
  6. Training (virtual environment, screenshots, video demos) → UAT / usability studies, then maintain after release.
