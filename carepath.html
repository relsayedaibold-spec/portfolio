Not sure which doctor to see first?

CarePath AI helps you figure out the right starting point based on your symptoms — safely, and without guessing. It's not a diagnosis. It's a map to the right care, with citations, confidence scores, and a clear escalation rule when something looks urgent.

01 / The system

Six layers. One navigation engine.

Every recommendation is traceable, uncertainty-aware, and bounded by safety rules a licensed clinician would recognize.

01 · Problem

The gap before the appointment.

Patients struggle to navigate where to go first. Search-driven self-diagnosis is anxiety-inducing and frequently wrong. The gap costs time, money, and outcomes.

02 · Solution

Clinical navigation, not diagnosis.

AI maps symptom cluster → diagnostic action → specialist category. Designed to point you at the right starting line, not replace the clinician.

03 · Non-diagnostic AI

Explainable. Bounded.

Models decision pathways with safety-constrained, explainable recommendations. Says "I don't know — see a doctor" when it doesn't know.

04 · Wearable signals

Continuous vitals layer.

Real-time heart rate, HRV, SpO₂, sleep, and activity feed contextual health data into the navigation model. Anomaly detection runs in the background.

05 · Research collaboration

Uncertainty-aware reasoning.

Built with Santa Clara University. Focus on explainable clinical decision modeling, calibrated confidence, and hard safety boundaries.

06 · Infrastructure potential

Hospital + clinic intake.

Designed as infrastructure for hospital intake, triage teams, multi-specialty clinics, and digital healthcare platforms. Not a consumer chatbot.

⌚ Wearable device integration layer

CarePath integrates with wearable health devices to track continuous vital signals, detect abnormalities in real time, and identify longitudinal patterns — enabling proactive clinical navigation before symptoms escalate.
